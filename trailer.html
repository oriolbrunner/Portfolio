NO MERCY TRAILER

This was probably the one of the projects where i had the most draft since my goal was to make the trailer atleast 2 minutes long, but with the limited footage it was very hard to make the trailer captivating and interesting so i wouldn't loose the viewers interest in those 2 minutes.
