SHOWREEL 2021

This little showreel was a teaser for a sports event collaborated by GO-ES.TV. In this teaser I decided to demonstrate the 3 sports that were going to take place at the event, Skate, Surf and Snowboard because the video had to be a maximum of 30 seconds. The graphics / animation were created in After Effects and the rest of the video in Premiere

Softwares used for this project;

  • Premiere pro
  • After Effects
  • Photoshop
